# Executive Summary

This dashboard provides a leadership-ready view of organizational performance using SQL-prepared operational data and Power BI reporting.

## Key Findings

- Total operational volume is approximately 2K records.
- On-time performance is strong at 86%.
- Average delay is approximately 1.02 days.
- Risk segmentation shows High, Medium, and Low risk groups.
- Data quality checks show no missing values in key operational fields.

## Business Value

The dashboard helps decision-makers identify operational drivers, monitor delivery performance, investigate bottlenecks, and validate dataset reliability before making business decisions.

## Recommendations

- Monitor high-risk routes and destinations regularly.
- Review disruption events with the highest delay impact.
- Use data quality checks before dashboard refreshes.
- Track shipping cost and delay together to identify expensive bottlenecks.
